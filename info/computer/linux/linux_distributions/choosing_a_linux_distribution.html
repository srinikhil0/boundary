Suppose you intend to buy a new car. What factors do you need to consider to make a proper choice?

The Requirements which need to be taken into account include the size needed to fit your family in the vehicle, the type of engine and gas economy, the mileage, the speed, your expected budget and available financing options, reliability record and after-sales services, etc.
